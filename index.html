We've been Making Delicious Meals Since 1979

Since we opened on 6th November 1979, our mission has been to serve the best dining experience to our customers, keeping in mind both health and tastes.

We serve various kinds of dishes ranging from sweet to spicy to salty and more. These delicacies serve to provide pleasant and wholesome experience to our customers.
